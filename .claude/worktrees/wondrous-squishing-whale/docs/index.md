features:
  - title: Executable Law
    details: Dutch legislation encoded in YAML, executed by a deterministic engine. Every decision is traceable to its legal source.
  - title: Cross-Law References
    details: Laws reference each other just as they do in statute books. The engine resolves dependencies automatically across the entire corpus.
  - title: Test-Driven Legislation
    details: BDD scenarios derived from legislative intent (Memorie van Toelichting) verify that machine-readable law matches the legislature's purpose.
  - title: Open Source
    details: Built by the Dutch Ministry of the Interior (MinBZK). All law, all tooling, all decisions — open and auditable.
